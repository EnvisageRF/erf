The Butterfly Project

This project aims to study the population dynamics of local butterfly species and their interactions with native flora. We aim to create a database of butterfly sightings and provide educational workshops to raise awareness about the importance of butterflies in our ecosystems.

Citizen Science Program

Through our Citizen Science Program, we engage local communities in data collection and monitoring of local wildlife. Participants are trained to observe and report sightings, contributing valuable data for ongoing research and conservation efforts.
