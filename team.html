Varna Sree Kumar

Founder & Ecologist

Dibyendu Biswas

Founder & Environmentalist

MSc Ecology and Environment Studies

Mr. Biswas has a diverse research background, having worked on Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), species distribution modeling (SDM), and the movement ecology of vultures. He has a strong inclination towards interdisciplinary approaches to environmental issues. His interests now extend to behavioral ecology and tackling lesser-discussed environmental challenges that have significant, yet often overlooked, impacts. He is also a strong advocate for the use and dissemination of free and open-source software (FOSS) and knowledge-sharing practices.

But wait, Dibyendu is the trickster god himself! He’s “burdened with glorious purpose” to save the environment, and who knows? Maybe he’ll use his charm to convince everyone to recycle more or plant a tree or two!

Zainab Khan

Wildlife Biologist

MSc

Zainab is a dynamic wildlife biologist passionate about avian ecology, behaviour, and endangered species conservation. Whether monitoring birds in urban areas or studying the elusive Forest Owlet in remote landscapes, Zainab thrives in green spaces, unravelling the mysteries of avian behavior and their habitat dynamics. Her work takes her from the heart of India’s tiger reserves to lesser-known, unprotected landscapes, where she dives deep into conservation challenges.

Beyond the field, Zainab channels her creativity into wildlife art, blending science with artistry to bring animals to life in a unique and engaging way. With a love for both research and storytelling, she’s dedicated to making conservation as inspiring as it is impactful.
